Who Are We?

A family owned small business that can be found tucked away between the busy streets of New York City. Coffee made with love, friendly staff and warm atmosphere are only some of the things we, the Doe sisters, pride ourselves on offering. Our goal when opening was to create a safe space for our customers where they could escape hectic day-to-day life and enjoy some good coffee in a cozy space - and we're pretty sure we've achieved that. 

Jodie Doe
Owner

Jodie is the person you see walking around the shop making sure every customer is having a great time. Together with her sister she brought the Coffee Doe idea to life, and managed to combine both her sustainable lifestyle and her love for the community in creating an authentic shop full of heart and personality. 

Petra Doe
Barista

If there's one thing Petra likes teaching people it's that coffee always tastes better if it's made with love. To her, coffee represents a way to connect above all else. That is exactly why she can be found behind the counter hand-crafting each individual coffee order with love and getting to know people in the process.
